# Corporate Chic Makeup

This course is conducted in Mandarin. Participant must be able to read and communicate in Chinese. OverviewPick-up the essential tips from the makeup artist of the stars!Taught by one of Singapore’s Top Makeup Artists, Cheryl Zhang, who will share her expertise in facial analysis and the different makeup techniques to enhance facial features. In this half-day workshop, you will learn“V” Shape face lifting massage techniqueDifferent face shapes and features analysisUnderstand the skin undertone and how to choose the right productsIntroduction of makeup products and toolsComplete a daily corporate chic makeup - from base, highlights, brows, eyeliners, blush and lipsticksAt the end of the workshop, students will also have a hands-on session to do a basic makeup based on their skin undertone and features. Duration4 HoursTime2:00pm to 6:00pmFees (Inclusive of 7% GST)
Application Fee: S$32.10 Course Fee: S$321.00 Trainer Profile
Cheryl Zhang is a celebrity makeup artist with over 15 years of experience. She previously led the Mediacorp Studios’ Makeup Unit. From simple yet breathtaking makeup to head-turning red carpet looks, Cheryl oversaw a diverse makeup and hair requirement for artistes and news presenters appearing on all Mediacorp Chinese, English, and News channels. She was a key figure behind memorable large-scale ‘live’ shows such as Star Search and Star Awards, as well as a multitude of lifestyle programmes and drama serials.
