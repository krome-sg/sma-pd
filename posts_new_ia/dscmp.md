# Developing Stories for Cross Media Projects (Synchronous E-Learning)

## Overview

This practice-based workshop will guide the participants through the processes of creating characters, designing character's journeys and dynamics, developing plotlines, and structuring the narrative experience by the audience. By the end of this workshop, the participants would have formulated a show bible that will include character biographies, story arcs, episode stories, story world, etc. The show bible will serve as an important tool to help them communicate their creative vision and transform it into a script.

Dr Lee Chee Keng brings with him rich experience in conceptualising, writing, and teaching TV drama writing and has worked alongside numerous renowned TV drama writers in China. He is a long-time associate of Zhang Xiaofeng Writing Studio and has worked on works such as Seeking Nirvana at Shaolin (2016) and Pushing Hands (2019). After graduating from the National University of Singapore and the renowned Central Academy of Drama (Beijing), Chee Keng dedicated his time and energy to writing and teaching. He has taught at the Shanghai Theatre Academy, Yale-NUS College, the National University of Singapore, and the Nanyang Technological University.
