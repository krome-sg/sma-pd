# Professional Holiday Video-Making

OverviewJoin us at this 1-day workshop for anyone who has zero knowledge in making your own video. Get a hands-on introduction to video production that covers all the basic techniques of making a video with simple recording devices. You will learn how to create a storyboard, be introduced to film genres, gain a hands-on practice at shooting your own movie, and also learn how to do basic post-production editing. At the end of the workshop, you would have produced your own video for sharing.
