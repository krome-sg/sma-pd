# Your Future in the Media Industry

OverviewJoin us for this session with three distinguished professionals who will share with us how they entered the media industry and what it takes to succeed. Through them, find out how youths are riding on technology, social media, and digital trends to achieve success. Understand the future of the media industry and learn from media industry experts how they reinvent themselves. Discover how you can evolve and benefit from this media metamorphosis.
