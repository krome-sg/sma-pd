# Digital Marketing

## Overview

This course focuses on the holistic understanding of digital marketing – from concepts to creation to implementation, tracking, and optimisation. This course programme will cover the most up-to-date digital marketing practices, tips, frameworks, and insights to help adult learners apply their learnings to their businesses and professional jobs.
## Learning Outcomes

- Acquire the basic concepts, terminology, and appreciation around the domain of Digital Marketing
- Establish a good foundation on the right path to further acquire knowledge and experience in the practice of Digital Marketing
- Understand how to choose the right digital marketing channels to meet business objectives
- Develop a strategic mindset that integrates digital marketing into their respective functions
## Who Should Attend

Marketing & Branding Professional, Programming Manager, Business Manager, New Media Sales Professional
