# Camera Presence for Business Leaders

OverviewA practical intensive training course that aims to ground the participant, who presents his/her organisation to both internal stakeholders and the wider world, with public speaking skills. Strategies and techniques that will enable one to be at ease and effective in a variety of settings. Learning OutcomesEstablish a BaselineCrafting the SpeechFine Tuning the MessageDurationHalf-Day (1 to 3 pax) Full-Day (4 to 6 pax)
