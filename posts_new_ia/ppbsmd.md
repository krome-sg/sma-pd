# * Plan the Production Budget, Schedule and Methods of Delivery

## Overview

This course covers the planning of the production budget for film production. The skills include preparing the production budget, the production schedule, and the main methods of delivery, managing and monitoring ongoing production expenditure while balancing creative expectations and working knowledge of reporting procedures, and the various strategies for revising financial targets and performance.
## Learning Outcomes

- Execute due diligence on the project by evaluating the script and script elements
- Create a budget/financial plan outlining the project costs required to develop and deliver the project
- Supervise the hiring of the cast and crew and create a production schedule based on delivery requirements
- Monitor the production pace to ensure that the production remains on schedule
- Monitor the financial status of the production to ensure that the production costs remain within budget
- Manage post-production elements to ensure the completion of the production
