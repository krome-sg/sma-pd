# SGUS - Media Writing Certificate Programme (Copy)

FULL-TIME STUDY ONLY  |
## Course Fee
SUBSIDY  |  S$1,200 MONTHLY TRAINING ALLOWANCE
## Funding Eligibility
- Criteria
- A Singapore Citizen or Singapore Permanent Resident;At least aged 21 and above;Did not graduate or is not scheduled to graduate in calendar years 2019 or later from any ITE or tertiary education institution (this includes polytechnics and universities, whether local or foreign);[For Male Applicants Only] Did not complete or is not scheduled to complete National Service in calendar years 2019 or later;Did not undertake or is not currently undertaking a different course which was part of the SGUnited Skills Programme or the SGUnited Mid-Career Pathways Programme – Company Training;Is not currently undertaking a programme which was part of the SGUnited Traineeships Programme, SGUnited Mid-Career Pathways Programme – Company Attachment or Career Conversion Programme;Can commit to full-time training (9am to 6pm on weekdays & some weekends) and meet the 75% minimum attendance requirement for every module; and
- Must complete and pass all module assessments to be eligible for the monthly training allowance.
Please be informed that in-person classes organised by Singapore Media Academy may take place at venues where vaccinated-differentiated measures are in place (e.g., Mediacorp Campus). In line with the Government’s SMM, with effect from 15 January 2022, persons who are not vaccinated will not be allowed entry into these premises.
A full-time training programme in writing that imparts skills and knowledge covering the key forms of media writing like narratives, sitcoms, and documentaries. In addition, attendees will also learn key topics like IP and copyrights and fundamental digital skills like getting headhunted online, digital marketing, and digital photography and videography.

Career Prospects
Copy Writers, Digital Media Executives, Documentary Writers, Editorial Assistants, Feature Journalists, Playwrights, Research Writers, Screenwriters, Scriptwriters, Staff Writers and Story Editors
Course Modules

Write a Narrative Script
Write a Documentary Script
Write a Sitcom Script
Getting Headhunted Online
Administer and Manage the Use of Licensed Copyright Materials Created
Fundamentals of a Professional in a Digital Workplace
Digital Marketing Using Photos and Videos
Breakdown a Script

## Mode of Training
Theory (Blended Learning - Virtual Classes and In-Person Training)
## Assessment

Some modules will conclude with a compulsory assessment component.
Assessments may include role plays, quizzes, assignments, projects, and written examinations.
Participants need to achieve at least 75% attendance for each module before they are allowed to sit for the assessment.

## Certification
Participants will receive a Certificate of Attendance issued by Singapore Media Academy for each module completed.
Completion of all the required modules will qualify candidates for the Certificate of Completion in SGUS - Media Writing Certificate Programme.
Course Duration and Schedule
Full-Time Programme (6 months)

Next Intake: 2 March 2022 (Registration closes on 15 February 2022)

For Online Classes and Modular Assignments: Trainees need to have -

Laptops or Desktops with a functional webcam and basic Microsoft Office applications.

Fee (Inclusive of 7% GST)
Nett Course Fee after Subsidy: S$500.00 (SkillsFuture Credits eligible)
## Minimum Entry Requirements

Academic Requirement: Obtain Pass Grade in GCE O-Level subjects including at least B4 for English (or IELTS overall band score 6.5 and above or equivalent) and 2 other subjects or equivalent
For applicants who do not meet the minimum requirements may still apply and will be considered based on:
At least 1 year working experience in any industry; and/or
- Oral interview or evaluation test by Singapore Media Academy to determine suitability for the course
- Applicants may be called for interview at the discretion of Singapore Media Academy.
