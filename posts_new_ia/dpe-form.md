# Developing the Pilot Episode Enquiry

Express your interest by sharing your details, and we will connect to provide the course details.

MasterclassDeveloping the Pilot Episode (Synchronous E-Learning) In this project-based workshop, participants will build on a series bible and outline to write a complete pilot episode of TV or web series - drama, comedy, action, or horror. Participants will learn and work on components of the creative process and acquire a structured approach that will help produce a pitch-ready script. Topics covered in this workshop include setting up scenes and sequences, moving the story with character action and dialogue, structuring and outlining episodes. By the end of this workshop, participants would have written a complete pilot episode and be ready to start on the next episode. This is a 30-hour training programme.
