# * Creative Storytelling – Developing Storylines

## Overview

In this 2-day course, participants will immerse themselves in the art of narrative filmmaking, honing their skills to craft compelling stories and dynamic characters. Through a comprehensive exploration of story development, participants will learn to define plots and characters with clarity, while also generating innovative ideas and themes to infuse depth into their narratives. Participants will master the art of crafting professional scripts that captivate audiences. Furthermore, the course will equip learners with a diverse toolkit of visual storytelling techniques, enabling them to effectively convey meaning, context, and emotion within their stories. Through a combination of theoretical insights and practical exercises, participants will emerge with the expertise needed to produce engaging and impactful narrative films.
## Learning Outcomes

- Generate ideas for a narrative film, create a theme for a story and build effective story plots
- Develop engaging and believable characters and write a script in a professional format
- Utilise various visual storytelling techniques to convey meaning, context, and emotion in a story

## Who Should Attend

Screenwriter (Film), Staff Writer (TV), Writer (Entertainment), Director, Producer, Content Creators, Vlogger, Aspiring Writer, Marketing/Communications Professional, Social/Digital Marketer
