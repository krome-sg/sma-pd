# * Brand Management

## Overview

The course delves into essential strategies for creating compelling brand identities that resonate deeply with both consumers and stakeholders. Participants will gain a solid grasp of fundamental branding principles and effective approaches for executing successful branding campaigns. Through a blend of theoretical frameworks, illuminating case studies, interactive group discussions, and immersive hands-on exercises, the course is designed to provide a highly practical learning experience. Participants are also encouraged to bring real branding scenarios and challenges to the sessions, fostering a rich and dynamic learning environment.
## Learning Outcomes

- Understand Branding Fundamentals
- Analyse Stakeholder Influence in Branding
- Utilise Social Media and Influencers in Branding
- Develop Customer-Centric Branding Strategies
- Design and Execute Branding Campaigns
- Enhance Brand Identity and Reputation
- Comprehend Legal Aspects of Branding
- Plan Effective Public Relations Strategies

## Who Should Attend

Marketing and Branding Professional, Product Manager, Entrepreneurs and Business Owner, Content Creator, Influencer, Digital/ Social Media Marketer, and Professional working in communication, public relations, and advertising industries
