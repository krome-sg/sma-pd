# * Effective Presentations and Pitches in a Virtual Environment

## Overview

This course focuses on developing effective presentation techniques, adapting to the virtual environment, and mastering virtual communication tools. Participants learn how to engage and persuade virtual audiences, create visually appealing presentations, and handle technical challenges that may arise during virtual presentations. This course incorporates practical exercises, interactive discussions, and feedback sessions to enhance the learning experience.
## Learning Outcomes

- Introduction to Virtual Presentations
- Mastering Power
- Point for Virtual Presentations
- Utilising OBS for Virtual Presentations
- Engaging Virtual Audiences
- Delivering Persuasive Virtual Pitches
- Enhancing Virtual Presence and Body Language
- Mastering Zoom Features for Virtual Presentations
- Using OBS with You
- Tube and Facebook
- Handling Technical Challenges

## Who Should Attend

Business Professionals, Entrepreneurs, Sales & Business Development Teams, marketing and communications professionals, Students & Educators. Any working professional who engage stakeholders online looking to strengthen their presentation skills in a virtual environment.
