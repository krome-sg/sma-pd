# * Develop and Manage Strategic Plan for a Digital Media Product

## Overview

This course covers the outcomes required to contribute to developing and implementing a strategic plan for a digital media product. This includes developing a strategic plan to meets the requirements of the client, audience, and/or company, establish the system design, perform quality assurance and user acceptance testing, and implementing the application.
## Learning Outcomes

- Develop strategic plan for digital media product to meet needs of client, audience, or company
- Gather and analyse client or user requirements for each module to design systems diagrams for the digital media product
- Oversee systems design and application to manage deliverables for each module
- Perform quality assurance and user acceptance testing for each module to ensure application meets requirements
- Implement application in the appropriate production environment, ensuring that the application works as designed

## Who Should Attend

Creative Director, Project Manager & Producer, Information Architect, Chief Technology Officer, Business Development Representative, Quality Assurance Analyst
