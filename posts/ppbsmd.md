OVERVIEWThis course covers the planning of the production budget for film production. The skills include preparing the production budget, the production schedule, and the main methods of delivery, managing and monitoring ongoing production expenditure while balancing creative expectations and working knowledge of reporting procedures, and the various strategies for revising financial targets and performance.LEARNING OUTCOMESExecute due diligence on the project by evaluating the script and script elementsCreate a budget/financial plan outlining the project costs required to develop and deliver the projectSupervise the hiring of the cast and crew and create a production schedule based on delivery requirementsMonitor the production pace to ensure that the production remains on scheduleMonitor the financial status of the production to ensure that the production costs remain within budgetManage post-production elements to ensure the completion of the productionWHO SHOULD ATTENDProducer, Assistant Producer, Production AssistantMODE OF TRAININGIn-PersonDURATION25 HoursDATETraining: October 2025 (TBC)Assessment: -Registration closes on -.Note:This course will conclude with a compulsory assessment.
