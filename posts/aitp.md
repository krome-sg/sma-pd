OVERVIEWComplete your accreditation as an Adult Educator (AE) for the media industry in 40 hours.This Accelerated Industry-centric Training Pathway (AITP) is a specially curated programme with the Institute for Adult Learning (IAL), and recognised by SkillsFuture Singapore (SSG) for purposes of meeting SSG Adult Educator (AE) requirements for the Media Sector. This programme is designed by IAL to uphold the highest standards in adult learning design and delivery.Singapore Media Academy is the Appointed Industry Partner (AIP) to deliver this programme that is contextualised specifically for the Media sector. It serves to accredit trainers, enabling them to deliver SSG-funded Training and Adult Education (TAE) courses within the Media domain. Unlike the generic WSQ ACLP qualification, the AITP is industry specific— accrediting learners to teach/facilitate only within their respective domain of contextualisation.This intensive programme spans 40 hours over 5 days and equips participants with the knowledge and competencies needed to deliver domain-relevant WSQ courses effectively.LEARNING OUTCOMESDesign & Planning Analyse learner and organisational needsTailor learning, assessment & coaching plansEngaging Delivery Facilitate with varied techniquesUse questioning & communication methodsAdapt delivery based on feedbackTechnology & Tools Leverage technology for learning & coachingEnhance collaboration and assessmentAssessment & Feedback Conduct fair, ethical assessmentsRecord, review, and ensure consistencyProvide feedback for improvementLearner Environment Manage group dynamicsFoster inclusivity and collaborationStakeholder Engagement Set expectations, align with goalsEvaluate effectiveness with dataWHO SHOULD ATTENDCurrent media professionals aspiring to be media adult educators.
