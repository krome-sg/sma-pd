OVERVIEWThe course delves into essential strategies for creating compelling brand identities that resonate deeply with both consumers and stakeholders. Participants will gain a solid grasp of fundamental branding principles and effective approaches for executing successful branding campaigns.Through a blend of theoretical frameworks, illuminating case studies, interactive group discussions, and immersive hands-on exercises, the course is designed to provide a highly practical learning experience.
