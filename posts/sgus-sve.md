Please be informed that in-person classes organised by Singapore Media Academy may take place at venues where vaccinated-differentiated measures are in place (e.g., Mediacorp Campus). In line with the Government’s SMM, with effect from 15 January 2022, persons who are not vaccinated will not be allowed entry into the premises.
A full-time training programme in video and audio editing spanning import, editing, and manipulation of visual and sound in post-production process using industry level softwares. Attendees will learn to make creative decisions in editing video and audio as well as ensuring quality and integrity of video and sound in addition to camera requirements of production shoots.

Career Prospects
Assistant Producers, Trailer Producers, Video Editors and Online Content Editors
Course Modules

Create Technical Editing Decisions 
Import, Edit and Export Audio and Video Footage
Make Creative Editing Decisions for Moving Images
Edit Sound Using a Digital Audio Workstation
Ensure the Quality of Sound Recording
Breakdown a Script 
Coordinates Film or Television Camera Requirements on a Production Shoot

Mode of Training
Theory & Practical (Blended Learning - Virtual Classes and In-Person Training)
Assessment

Some modules will conclude with a compulsory assessment component.
Assessments may include role plays, quizzes, assignments, projects, and written examinations.
Participants need to achieve at least 75% attendance for each module before they are allowed to sit for the assessment.

Certification
Participants will receive a Certificate of Attendance issued by Singapore Media Academy for each module completed.
Completion of all the required modules will qualify candidates for the Certificate of Completion in SGUS - Sound and Video Editing Certificate Programme.
Course Duration and Schedule
Full-Time Programme (6 months)
