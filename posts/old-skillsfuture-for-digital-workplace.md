SkillsFuture for Digital Workplace is a national initiative that aims to equip Singaporeans with the mindset and basic functional skills to prepare for the future economy.Singapore Media Academy offers SFDW programmes for individuals acquiring digital skills at a foundational level and familiarising themselves with the various digital tools in the workplace and their daily lives. The courses cover topics in mobile photography & videography, fundamental skills in digital marketing, personal branding, and digital trends & the changing digital requirements.The full fee for each course is S$642.00; Singapore Citizens and Permanent Residents can enjoy SSG Funding and only need to pay a course fee of S$50. Terms and conditions apply.

+ Getting Headhunted Online
Overview

This 2-day SFDW course covers the concepts of personal branding, social selling, and how to apply them to increase the chances of getting headhunted online. The course also provides a basic understanding of how a LinkedIn Profile that can be found and followed by recruiters is created in addition to fundamental skills of digital marketing and the tools used in the online platforms.

Digital Marketing Landscape

+ Fundamentals of a Professional in a Digital Workplace
Overview

This 2-day SFDW course will cover fundamental skills in today’s digital workplace.
Topics like digital trends and changing digital requirements, functional and useful apps that will boost productivity, discerning what constitutes fake news, guarding the interest of your company against data breaches and cybersecurity will be included.
The key topics are:

Understand the work trends and job requirements of the future
Navigate through popular and useful apps in your daily life
Understand the importance of cybersecurity in daily and work applications
Data analytics and how data can be used at work
Identify fake news
Embrace learning and upskilling
