SkillsFuture for Digital Workplace is a national initiative that aims to equip Singaporeans with the mindset and basic functional skills to prepare for the future economy.Singapore Media Academy offers SFDW programmes for individuals acquiring digital skills at a foundational level and familiarising themselves with the various digital tools in the workplace and their daily lives. The courses cover topics in mobile photography & videography, fundamental skills in digital marketing, personal branding, and digital trends & the changing digital requirements.The full fee for each course is S$642.00; Singapore Citizens, Permanent Residents and Long Term Visit Pass Plus Holders can enjoy SSG Funding and only need to pay a course fee of S$50. The nett course fee is NETF (NTUC-Education and Training Fund) eligible. Terms and conditions apply.
