OVERVIEWThis course covers the outcomes required to contribute to developing and implementing a strategic plan for a digital media product. This includes developing a strategic plan to meets the requirements of the client, audience, and/or company, establish the system design, perform quality assurance and user acceptance testing, and implementing the application.LEARNING OUTCOMESDevelop strategic plan for digital media product to meet needs of client, audience, or companyGather and analyse client or user requirements for each module to design systems diagrams for the digital media productOversee systems design and application to manage deliverables for each modulePerform quality assurance and user acceptance testing for each module to ensure application meets requirementsImplement application in the appropriate production environment, ensuring that the application works as designedWHO SHOULD ATTENDCreative Director, Project Manager & Producer, Information Architect, Chief Technology Officer, Business Development Representative, Quality Assurance AnalystMODE OF TRAININGIn-Person and/or OnlineDURATION33 HoursDATETraining: 7 October 2025Assessment: 27 to 29 November 2025 Note: Assessment duration is one hour between 9am to 6pmRegistration closes on 23 September 2025 .Note:This course will conclude with a compulsory assessment.
