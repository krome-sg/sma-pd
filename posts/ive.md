OVERVIEWThis course covers editing immersive video content to achieve the creative vision and intended immersive experience for the audience. Participants will learn the features and functions of editing software, principles, and techniques of non-linear editing, colour, and lighting.The course will also cover the applications of rotoscoping, manipulation of visual parameters to create an immersive effect, and stitching techniques.LEARNING OUTCOMESAnalyse immersive video content to identify distortions and aberrationsPerform edits on immersive video content to achieve the creative vision and desired immersive experiencesExecute transitions to retain the seamlessness and coherence of immersive video contentStitch the immersive video content to remove distortions and aberrationsSynchronise pictures and sounds using time codes and frame ratesUpdate immersive video content to reflect additional editing decisions from creative leadership teamsWHO SHOULD ATTENDVideo Editor, Assistant Director, Assistant Editor, Production Assistant, Assistant Producer, Producer, Individuals who are keen to join the media industry MODE OF TRAININGIn-Person or OnlineDURATION25 HoursDATETraining: 5 January 2026Assessment: 30 January 2026Registration closes on 22 December 2025.Note:This course will conclude with a compulsory assessment.
