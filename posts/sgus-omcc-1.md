FULL-TIME STUDY ONLY  |  COURSE FEE SUBSIDY  |  S$1,200 MONTHLY TRAINING ALLOWANCEFunding Eligibility CriteriaA Singapore Citizen or Singapore Permanent Resident;At least aged 21 and above;Did not graduate or is not scheduled to graduate in calendar years 2019 or later from any ITE or tertiary education institution (this includes polytechnics and universities, whether local or foreign);[For Male Applicants Only] Did not complete or is not scheduled to complete National Service in calendar years 2019 or later;Did not undertake or is not currently undertaking a different course which was part of the SGUnited Skills Programme or the SGUnited Mid-Career Pathways Programme – Company Training;Is not currently undertaking a programme which was part of the SGUnited Traineeships Programme, SGUnited Mid-Career Pathways Programme – Company Attachment or Career Conversion Programme;Can commit to full-time training (9am to 6pm on weekdays & some weekends) and meet the 75% minimum attendance requirement for every module; andMust complete and pass all module assessments to be eligible for the monthly training allowance.
Please be informed that in-person classes organised by Singapore Media Academy may take place at venues where vaccinated-differentiated measures are in place (e.g., Mediacorp Campus). In line with the Government’s SMM, with effect from 15 January 2022, persons who are not vaccinated will not be allowed entry into these premises.
A full-time training programme in social media marketing that trains attendees in social media and digital marketing using suitable online platforms and tracking campaign performance through analytics. Attendees will also learn how to create, produce, and edit content using mobile technology on top of key issues like ethics, crisis management, and online engagement.

Career Prospects
Digital Marketers, Influencers, Online Content Creators, Social Media Marketers and Vloggers
Course Modules

Making Online Content
Promote Products and Services on Social Media
Social Media - Monitoring, Metrics and Analytics
Social Media - Public Relations, Ethics and Crisis Management
Manage IP at a Strategic Level
Write a Concept Proposal to Pitch to a Client
Digital Marketing Using Photos and Videos
Getting Headhunted Online

Mode of Training
Theory (Blended Learning - Virtual Classes and In-Person Training)
Assessment

Some modules will conclude with a compulsory assessment component.
Assessments may include role plays, quizzes, assignments, projects, and written examinations.
Participants need to achieve at least 75% attendance for each module before they are allowed to sit for the assessment.

Certification
Participants will receive a Certificate of Attendance issued by Singapore Media Academy for each module completed.
Completion of all the required modules will qualify candidates for the Certificate of Completion in SGUS - Online Marketing and Content Creation Certificate Programme.
Course Duration and Schedule
Full-Time Programme (6 months)

Next Intake: 1 March 2022 (Registration closes on 15 February 2022)

For Online Classes and Modular Assignments: Trainees need to have -

Laptops or Desktops with a functional webcam and basic Microsoft Office applications.

Fee (Inclusive of 7% GST)
Nett Course Fee after Subsidy: S$500.00 (SkillsFuture Credits eligible)
Minimum Entry Requirements

Academic Requirement: Obtain Pass Grade in GCE O-Level subjects including at least B4 for English (or IELTS overall band score 6.5 and above or equivalent) and 2 other subjects or equivalent
For applicants who do not meet the minimum requirements may still apply and will be considered based on:
At least 1 year working experience in any industry; and/or
Oral interview or evaluation test by Singapore Media Academy to determine suitability for the courseApplicants may be called for interview at the discretion of Singapore Media Academy.
