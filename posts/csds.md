OVERVIEWIn this 2-day course, participants will immerse themselves in the art of narrative filmmaking, honing their skills to craft compelling stories and dynamic characters. Through a comprehensive exploration of story development, participants will learn to define plots and characters with clarity, while also generating innovative ideas and themes to infuse depth into their narratives. Participants will master the art of crafting professional scripts that captivate audiences. Furthermore, the course will equip learners with a diverse toolkit of visual storytelling techniques, enabling them to effectively convey meaning, context, and emotion within their stories. Through a combination of theoretical insights and practical exercises, participants will emerge with the expertise needed to produce engaging and impactful narrative films.LEARNING OUTCOMESGenerate ideas for a narrative film, create a theme for a story and build effective story plotsDevelop engaging and believable characters and write a script in a professional formatUtilise various visual storytelling techniques to convey meaning, context, and emotion in a storyWHO SHOULD ATTENDScreenwriter (Film), Staff Writer (TV), Writer (Entertainment), Director, Producer, Content Creators, Vlogger, Aspiring Writer, Marketing/Communications Professional, Social/Digital MarketerMODE OF TRAININGIn-PersonDURATION16 HoursDATETraining: 21 and 22 Nov 2025 from 9am to 6pm (In person – Classroom)Assessment: 28 Nov 2025 (In person – Classroom)Note: Assessment duration: one hour between 9am to 6pmNote:This course will conclude with a compulsory assessment.
